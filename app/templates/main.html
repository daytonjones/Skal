Skålis an Old Norse word meaning bowl. From the tradition of passing the bowl of mead or beer,
the term “skål” is now also a toast — ”cheers!”
  • Use the Navigation Bar at the top to access the different features of Skål. You can calculate your ABV, Add/Edit/View your recipes (and batches you've made)...
  • If you have a Tilt Hydrometer, and use Tilt Pi to track your batches, you can access the Google Sheets generated/shared with you. This will require a little extra setup (one time) from you - namely creating an "app" and api key to access your shared documents.

    How to connect your TiltPi Sheets to Skål

    1. Go to the Google Cloud Console and create a new project.
    2. Navigate to the "API & Services" section and click "Enable APIs and Services".
    3. Search for "Google Drive API" and "Google Sheets" and enable them for your project.
    4. In the "Credentials" section, create OAuth 2.0 credentials.
    5. Download the credentials.json file and save it securely.
    6. Make sure you "publish" your newly created app
    7. Access the "TiltPi" page via the navbar and upload the credentials.json file when prompted, then follow the links to activate/approve the access.
